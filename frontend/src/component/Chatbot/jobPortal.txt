The website is an online job portal built using the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, and Node.js) to connect job seekers with recruiters. Authentication is handled using JWT (JSON Web Token) and Passport.js, ensuring secure login and user verification. Users authenticate with an email and password, and upon successful login, a JWT token is issued, granting access to protected routes based on user roles such as recruiter or applicant. If a user forgets their password, they can reset it by providing their email, which triggers an email with a link to change the password. Once an applicant logs in, they can apply for jobs, track all applications with their statuses, build a resume, and update their profile, including their resume and profile image. Applicants can also rate jobs, with the overall rating being calculated as the average of all ratings provided by different applicants. On the recruiter side, recruiters can create job postings and list them based on timelines, update job details, and shortlist candidates based on their qualifications. Recruiters can also update the status of applications and conduct video calls for all shortlisted candidates. Shortlisted candidates receive an email with the date and time of their interview, while rejected applicants are also notified via email. Additionally, recruiters can update their profiles, including their experience and company details. All accepted applicants receive a brief email summarizing the jobs they have applied for. Recruiters also provide ratings for employees working in their company. All interviews are scheduled between 10 AM and 1 PM or between 3 PM and 6 PM. All the technical and non-technical jobs will be listed in the portal. The backend, built with Node.js and Express.js, manages business logic, user authentication, and interactions with MongoDB, where user and job-related data are stored. The frontend, developed with React.js, communicates with the backend through API requests, ensuring a seamless user experience. The website is deployed using Render for both frontend and backend hosting, with backend API endpoints updated accordingly. The final application is accessible online, providing a scalable and efficient job portal solution.
